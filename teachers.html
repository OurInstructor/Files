Why University Goers Need To Join Us

Need of Hour is Creating Young Professionals Earning To Learn Basic Work Ethics and Having Financial Independence

 Western Students earn while they study. This is still quite a taboo in India as not all the part-time income sources are reputed and easily accessible for university students like working in a restaurant or departmental store . Students in other parts of the world work for limited hours and are able to earn for their decent expenses, all this during graduation days. This particular work culture amongst western students have immensely helped students improve their time management skills as well as the  work ethics. In India Most of the students rely on their parents money till the time they get a job which is why we have less no. of Young Professionals as compared to other countries. We are aiming to create a Student's Teaching Work Force which will earn and share their knowledge with Future University Goers.
